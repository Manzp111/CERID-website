🌍 About Us – Who We Are

At CERID (Centre d’Étude et de Recherche pour l’Innovation et le Développement), we believe that lasting change begins with people — empowered, educated, and inspired. Since our founding, we’ve been committed to transforming lives and communities through impactful projects in education, health, environment, and community empowerment. Driven by a passionate and diverse team, and backed by strong values of integrity, transparency, and solidarity, CERID collaborates with local and international partners to design innovative, sustainable solutions tailored to the real needs on the ground. Our mission is clear: “To promote inclusive development by empowering communities through research, innovation, and action.” From remote villages to urban neighborhoods, our projects bring hope, dignity, and opportunity to those who need it most. Join us as we work toward a world where every individual has the tools to build a better future.

Our Mission

To empower communities through innovative solutions, education, and sustainable development, guided by our core values of integrity, transparency, inclusivity, and compassion.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by visionary humanitarian Dr. Gilbert, our NGO started as a grassroots initiative addressing local education gaps. Over the years, it has evolved into a national force for social good, supporting thousands across multiple sectors.
