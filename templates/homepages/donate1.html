Why Donate?

Your support changes lives. Every coin you give helps us bring clean water, education, food, and hope to those who need it most. Together, we make a real, lasting impact.

Where the Money Goes

We believe in full honesty and transparency. That’s why:

  • ✔ 90% of your donation goes directly to helping people.
  • ✔ 10% helps cover things like transportation, tools, and staff.
  • ✔ You can always ask for our financial reports.

Types of Donations

🎁 One-Time Donation

Give once, when you can. Every gift counts and brings change.

🔄 Monthly Support

Help us every month. It keeps our mission strong and steady.

🎒 In-Kind Gifts

Donate clothes, books, or other useful items. We pass them to those in need.

🏢 Corporate Sponsorship

Companies can partner with us, support the community, and grow their positive image.
